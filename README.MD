6. About (Short Description / Summary): 
	It is a two player game. The turn of the player will be displayed on the top. The player can move to one adjacent column. 
	If the opponent is present in the nearest column and the other column is free then the player can jump over to that column and the opponent will be disappeared And the score will be incremented by 10. 
	The apples that are completely removed from the field will be lost the game.
